GOODS & SERVICES TAX REGISTRATION

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a uniform indirect tax levied on goods and services across the country. GST, as an umbrella tax, replaced central taxes such as VAT, Service Tax, central excise, additional and special duty of excise and customs etc., on supply of goods and services.

PERMANENT ACCOUNT NUMBER (PAN)

A permanent account number or PAN is a ten-digit alphanumeric identifier issued in form of a laminated card by the Income Tax Department of India to the Indian Tax payers and is unique to each individual. IT Department keeps a detailed record of all major transactions with PAN Number.
